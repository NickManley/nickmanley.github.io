Employment History

Ruxter

May 2012 — August 2012

Ruxter is a web based tool that allows users to create mobile websites with a simple, web based interface without writing code. It was designed for small business owners who want to market their business with a mobile website, but don't have the time or budget to afford an expensive web developer. Ruxter also has an SMS feature that let small business owners send out text messages to their own customers who subscribe to receive them.

I interned as a software developer at Ruxter during the summer of 2012. My job was to implement a new coupon feature so that business owners could create their own digital coupons to send to their customers. I did both front and backend work. The frontend made heavy use of jQuery. The backend was done using Java, Spring MVC 3, Hibernate, and MySQL.
